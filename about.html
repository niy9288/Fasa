Owner Profile

A welcoming corner for curious minds

Inspired by sunlit afternoons and the joy of discovery, The Palm Tree Library blends reading, art, and hands-on learning. Our mission is to nurture imagination and confidence through playful, purposeful experiences.

Alongside our reading programs, we also offer a curated selection of clothes from Abidjan, celebrating vibrant West African style and craftsmanship in our community.

Education philosophy: We believe in curiosity-led exploration, inclusive storytelling, and creative expression as foundations for lifelong learning.
